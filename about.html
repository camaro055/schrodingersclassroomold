The first ever online, interactive, educational quantum physics experience. Inspired by the brilliant minds you see in the photo above, Schrödinger's Classroom aims to immerse you in the world of physics like never before. You will explore the many diverse theories and experiments of quantum mechanics through our endless selection of lessons that involve readings, diagrams, flash games and much more! Once you are comfortable with the content, there are many challenging problems for you to solve and test your knowledge.

This website was originally created as a website design project by Cameron Naraine and Darren Strickland. Now it has grown into something much bigger.

Please enjoy Schödinger's Classroom! If you have any questions or concerns about the website, please contact (hyperlink "contact") us. We would love to hear your feedback!
